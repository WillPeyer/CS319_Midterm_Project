About Me

Hi, I'm Will Peyer, a Junior at Iowa State University studying Computer Science. I have a passion for both technology and cooking, which inspired me to create this Interactive Recipe Book.

My Background

As a Computer Science student, I've been exploring various aspects of software development. This project is part of my journey to combine my technical skills with my love for culinary arts.

The Project

This Interactive Recipe Book is a part of my SE/ComS319 Construction of User Interfaces course. It's designed to help people organize their recipes and discover new ones. I hope you find it useful and enjoyable!
